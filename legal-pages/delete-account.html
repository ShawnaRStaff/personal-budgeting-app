Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your Personal Budget account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account profile and email address
  • All transaction records
  • All accounts you've created
  • All budgets and budget history
  • All savings goals and contributions
  • All categories you've created
  • All recurring transaction settings

How to Request Deletion

To delete your account, please send an email to us with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

Request Account Deletion

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently deleted.
